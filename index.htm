Summary information about resource

The given scientific-educational portal –
information resource
was created by me as the really working author,
the head, the executor, the legal owner
and potential patent owner
of my unique cognitive modeling technology:
in technical sciences –
“The system analysis, control
and information processing” (05.13.01),
“Management in social
and economical systems” (05.13.10),
“Psycho-physiology of perception” (19.00.02),
“Psychology of work, engineering psychology
and ergonomics” (19.00.03),
in humanitarian and social sciences –
“Finance, monetary circulation
and credit” (08.00.10),
in natural sciences –
“Theoretical mechanics” (01.02.01),
“Physical chemistry” (02.00.04),
in medical sciences –
“Molecular biology” (03.00.03).

The given scientific-educational portal –
information resource
is represents of the interest
for scientists, teachers and experts:

1. In the field of the (microscopical)
system analysis,
information and communication
technologies,
were interested in a problematics
of the creation and operation
of the adaptive intellectual
information-educational environments,
using in the basis
of automated means of training
of the new generation,
taking into account individual features
of the personality of subject of training.

The cognitive modeling technology
for the system analysis
of information-education environment
(at microscopic level) is presented.

The sphere of scientific interests:
“(Cognitive) computer science”,
“Psypho-physiology of perception”,
“(Cognitive) psychology”
and “(Cognitive) linguistics”.

Methodical support
for students studying the discipline
“Computer science” is presented.

2. In the field of the (microscopical)
financial analysis
of (credit) organizations and enterprises,
were interested in a problematics
of creation and operation
of the information systems,
using in the basis
of the finance analysis automated means
of new generation,
taking into account individual
features of the economy subjects
and personnel.

The cognitive modeling technology
for the financial analysis
of (credit) organizations
(at microscopic level) is presented.

The sphere of scientific interests:
“(Cognitive) Computer science”,
“The financial analysis,
monetary circulation and credit”,
“Banking” and “Accounting and audit”.

Methodical support
for students studying the disciplines
“Finance, monetary circulation and credit”
and “The financial analysis and audit
of (credit) organization“ are presented.

3. In the field of the (microscopical)
complex analysis of difficult
objects, processes and phenomena,
were interested in a problematics
of creation and operation
of the information systems,
using in the basis
of the complex analysis automated means
of new generation,
taking into account individual
features of difficult objects,
processes, phenomena and personnel.

The cognitive modeling technology
for the complex analysis
of difficult objects, processes and phenomena
(at microscopic level) is presented.

Sphere of scientific interests:
“(Cognitive) Computer science”,
“Theoretical mechanics”,
“Physical chemistry”
and “Molecular biology”.

Methodical support
for students studying disciplines
“The complex analysis”,
“Technologies, methods and algorithms
of the complex analysis”
and “The complex analysis
automation means” are presented.

Modern painting, architecture,
design and art

“The Suprematism” –
a modern direction in painting,
which allows to generate
the difficult geometrical figure
on the plane by means of a set
of the simple geometrical figures
(К. Malevich).

 

“The Black square” –
concentration of the unidentified
in depth of two measurements
(К. Malevich).

“The Red square” –
a projection of the peasant woman
in depth of two measurements
(К. Malevich).

 

“The Cognitive volumetric suprematism”
(also “The Cognitive spherism”) –
the new direction in modern
painting, architecture,
design and art,
which allows to generate
the volumetric difficult graphic
picture by means of a set
of the simple flat and volumetric
geometrical figures in the Descartes
three-dimensional system of coordinates
(the method of eight points
and eight contours)
(Vetrov A.N.).

 

“The Cognitive black sphere” –
concentration of the ideal non-linear
and non-unique unidentified
in environment of three measurements
(Vetrov A.N.).

 

“The Cognitive red sphere” –
ideal non-linear and non-unique
model of “The Sun”
in environment of three measurements
(Vetrov A.N.).
