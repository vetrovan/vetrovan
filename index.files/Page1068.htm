The note:

1. The scientific research centres are created in territory of The RF and the foreign countries
only after the sanction and reception of the license of the author of the unique cognitive modeling technology
(for microscopic system, financial and complex analysis),
“The Government of The RF”, “The Russian author's society” (Moscow city, The RF)
(as agreed with “The Government of the foreign country” for non-residents).

2. The scientific research institutes are accepted in structure
of “The state international organization "Academy of cognitive natural sciences"”
only after the sanction and reception of the license of the author of unique cognitive modeling technology
(for microscopic system, financial and complex analysis),
“The Government of The RF”, “The Russian author's society” (Moscow city, The RF)
(as agreed with “The Government of the foreign country” for non-residents).

3. The settlement of disagreements and registration of the specified organizations is carried out.
